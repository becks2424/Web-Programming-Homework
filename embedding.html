Learning about Photography

Basics:

This webpage will primarily contain videos that can help
photography beginners find their way around using cameras.

Why?

The reason as to why I chose to look into YouTube videos, is because I would like for people
to use my website as platform as a starting point for people who are interested in learning about not only
learning about cameras, but photography as well. This makes it much more easier for them, instead of always going
on YouTube and trying to figure out which video would be best for them, they can just come to my website and find what
they're looking for.
