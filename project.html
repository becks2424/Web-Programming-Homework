MY LOVE FOR PHOTOGRAPHY

WHY PHOTOGRAPHY?:

My name is Rebecca Menda, I am a Computer Science major student at Graceland University.
I am originally from Zambia, Africa, and I am currently in the US for education purposes only. growing up, my father enjoyed taking pictures of the family on every occassion that he could, and for the longest I didn't realize how much of an impact those little moments had on me until I got a little older. After I got to Graceland, the surroundings helped me spend more time alone around nature and increased my appreciation of it's beauty. This in turn led to me taking pictures every now and then until it became a hobby.
