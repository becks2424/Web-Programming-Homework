My Lamoni Experience

Being in Lamoni has been an interesting experience for me so far. The first thing that stood out
to me when I first got here was just how friendly people of this community are. I did not imagine that people in this community would be
as friendly as they are, and that's what I appreciate about this town a lot. But with pros comes cons, and one of the cons of this city I
would say is the fact that they have no stores at all here, which makes it hard for students sometimes to find things to do, not only
that, but for international students like me, it makes it hard to live here without having a car. But overall, it's still a nice, safe
peaceful place to live in and I enjoy it whenever I get the chance to.
